MUSEUM OF POKEMON

Step inside the Pokémon Museum, where history and heart collide. Fossilized Aerodactyl wings hang above a replica of the first Poké Ball, cracked and glowing faintly. Murals trace the journey from ancient Ruins of Alph to modern-day Galar. A glass case holds a tattered hat, once worn by a legendary Trainer. Children press noses to exhibits of shimmering evolution stones. In the hushed halls, every badge, sketch, and footprint whispers the same truth: this isn't just a collection—it's a living tribute to bonds that never fade.

MUSEUM OF POKEMON

Welcome to the Pokémon TCG Museum—where every card tells a story. Floor-to-ceiling displays showcase vintage holographic Charizards, misprinted Blastoises, and rare trophy cards gleaming under soft light. Visitors pause before a shadowless Mewtwo, then drift to a wall of 1999 starter decks in mint condition. Interactive screens let you flip through digital binders of every expansion, from Base Set to Scarlet & Violet. In the center, a glass case holds the legendary Pikachu Illustrator—a one-of-a-kind treasure. Here, art meets nostalgia, and each shuffled memory feels like drawing an ace.
